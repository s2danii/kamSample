ABC Consulting Inc.

Partners in navigating complexity

Our story

Based in the General Toronto Area, we have successfully assisted our clients achieve what's important to them over the past three years.

How we help our clients

We work with our determined clients to help them fulfill their potential. We specialize in complex accounting, transaction advisory, senior level finance solutions and management consulting.

Why choose us

Our team comes from a public accounting background, developing a solid foundation in accounting and finance, allowing us to better support our clients.

Our services

Accounting Advisory Services

We have deep financial reporting experience across multiple industries in IFRS and US GAAP. We help companies solve complex accounting and financial reporting challenges by supporting companies in implementing new accounting standards, preparing and reviewing financial statements and other public documents.

IPO / RTO Services

We have significant experience with helping companies go public. We assist our clients in preparing for an IPO/RTO by evaluating and implementing the requirements for going public and reporting as a public company. We are there with our clients from the beginning of the process to ongoing compliance after becoming public.

CFO Services / Interim Management

Our team represents senior level qualified professionals who were CFO's/senior finance executives of well established companies and on average have over 12 years of experience with broad skill sets and diverse industry backgrounds. We offer specialized expertise on an as needed basis and can manage internal projects as well as support existing internal resources.
